# 🚀 ET Intelligence Agent Hub

### THE ULTIMATE AI-POWERED WEALTH WORKSPACE FOR THE INDIAN INVESTOR

**ET Intelligence** is a multi-agent AI financial ecosystem designed to decentralize institutional-grade market research for the retail investor. Built for the **ET AI Hackathon 2026**, this platform transforms fragmented data—from SEBI filings and NSE technicals to complex tax regimes—into a single, actionable intelligence layer.

## 🏆 Problem Statements Addressed

Our solution provides a comprehensive answer to multiple challenge tracks:
*   **PS-6 (Investor Intelligence)**: Real-time "Radar" for corporate filings and technical crossovers.
*   **PS-7 (AI Concierge)**: Personalized member onboarding and discovery paths.
*   **PS-8 (Enterprise Content Ops)**: Automated signal-to-briefing generation.
*   **PS-9 (AI Money Mentor)**: Sophisticated tax wizard and retirement simulations.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. 📡 Opportunity Radar (PS-6)
A high-velocity signal finder that scans the markets for:
- **Insider Trades**: Management accumulation alerts.
- **Technical Breakouts**: VWAP and 200-DMA crossovers.
- **Corporate Filings**: SEBI disclosure analysis in plain English.

### 2. 🧪 Market Analyst Lab
A technical research station with **TradingView Lightweight Charts (v5)**.
- **Sector Heatmap**: Visualizes the flow of institutional money across NSE sectors.
- **Pattern Detection**: Automated Bollinger Band and RSI alerts with agent-led reasoning.

### 3. 🧙 AI Money Mentor (PS-9)
Personalized financial guidance with zero jargon:
- **Tax Wizard 2026**: Side-by-side comparison of Old vs. New tax regimes.
- **FIRE Roadmap**: Real-time progress tracking toward Financial Independence.

### 4. 🎩 ET Concierge (PS-7)
A member-exclusive workspace:
- **Investor Profile**: Aggressive vs. Conservative bias profiling.
- **Discovery Engine**: AI-curated masterclasses and Wealth Summit recommendations.

## 🏛️ Technical Architecture

Built for scale and performance:
- **Framework**: Next.js 14+ (App Router).
- **Visualization**: Lightweight Charts (High-perf candlestick engine).
- **Identity**: Simulated Authentication Context for session persistence.
- **Design**: Premium Glassmorphism UI with Dark-Mode-First aesthetics.
